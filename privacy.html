Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

TradeReview AI is a private trading journal for recording trades, screenshots, notes, and review statistics. This policy explains what information the app may collect and how it is used.

Information we collect

  • Account information such as email address and sign-in provider.
  • Journal information you enter, including trades, symbols, notes, tags, screenshots, broker preferences, and onboarding answers.
  • Basic technical information needed to run the app, secure accounts, and troubleshoot issues.

How we use information

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To save, sync, and display your trading journal across devices.
  • To calculate journal statistics such as P/L, win rate, R-multiple, and mistake patterns.
  • To improve reliability, support, and future mobile app features.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your trading journal data.
  • We do not provide financial advice or trade recommendations.
  • We do not ask for brokerage passwords. Future broker sync should use approved broker APIs or secure OAuth-style access where available.

Service providers

TradeReview AI may use third-party infrastructure providers for authentication, database storage, hosting, analytics, or support. These services are used only to operate and improve the app.

Your choices

You can stop using the app at any time. If you need help with account or data deletion, open a support request and do not include passwords, authentication links, or private trade details in a public support message.

Trading risk

Trading involves substantial risk. TradeReview AI is a journaling and review tool only. It is not a broker, investment adviser, or financial adviser.

Contact

For support, open a request at GitHub Issues.
